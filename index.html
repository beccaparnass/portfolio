About Becca

My name is Becca, and I am a dedicated and responsible student studying journalism at Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland College Park.

I am currently a staff writer at Stories Beneath the Shell, a student-run digital publication. I pitch and cover weekly stories that are of importance to the student body, and am responsible for meeting strict deadlines.

In the spring, I will be interning at DC Witness, a media company that combines journalism with transparency regarding the D.C. criminal justice system. DC Witness reports on criminal cases as they progress through the D.C. Superior Court.

When I'm not working in journalism, I enjoy creative writing and reading, and spending time with my family and friends.
